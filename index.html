The Story of '14

By Kelvin Consuegra

"The story of '14" is a documentary styled based comic, in which we see the origins of Godzilla (the 2014 vrsion) in a post apocalyptic world where his species has been wiped out, and the only other surviving member of his species is an older Godzilla named Gojira. As these two set out on their journey to survive their enemies and the invasice species called the M.U.T.O's are still on the hunt to kill the last two of the Godzilla species.
